Trident Drone Pre-Order FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Purchasing, Charging Credit Cards, and Fulfillment:

How do I control Trident?
Trident can be controlled with a modern Android phone or tablet running Android 5.1 or later using the OpenROV app. We are working on an iOS app as well. We also offer a dedicated controller preinstalled with the OpenROV app on our accessories page.
What comes with Trident?
Your Trident will come with everything you need to use Trident with your own Android device:
  • cardboard carrying case
  • 25m neutrally buoyant tether
  • 25m tether spool
  • Topside WiFi
  • 110V-240V wall charger
  • trim weights for use in saltwater
    I want to buy a new tablet to control Trident, which one do you recommend?
    Trident can be controlled via the OpenROV App using a tablet with the following recommended specs:
    • Android 5.1 or later
    • At least 2GB RAM
    • At least a Quad-core 1.3Ghz
    • 802.11n wifi protocol. Dual-band preferred (5GHz)

    Not required but nice to have:
    • At least 7″, 720p resolution
    • Good screen/backlight brightness
    • Long battery life (>3 hours with full screen brightness and WiFi on)
    • Built-in GPS
    • SD or micro SD card slot

    Tablets we have already tested and like:
    • Low-end: ASUS ZenPad 8
    • Mid-range: Samsung Galaxy Tab S2 8.0
    • High-end: Google Pixel C

    Some users prefer an external gamepad rather than using a touchscreen. When selecting an external gamepad do keep in mind that the Bluetooth pairing may interfere with WiFi communication. If you notice this, try connecting your external controller via cable to your device.

    Please refer to the manufacturer for different compatibilities/capabilities, but we like these:

    Do I need WiFi to use Trident? What if I don’t have cell service?
    The Trident Topside creates its own WiFi network to send video and control signals between Trident and your device. You only need internet access to update your software or upload your videos.
    I have a great idea and I think it’s good enough to get some Tridents free or discounted.
    We designed Trident for people who have great ideas and need access to tools like Trident but don’t have the budget for commercial ROVs. Because we are already competitively priced, it’s not likely that we can give further discounts, but if you think we would be interested in hearing about your idea, send an email to support@openrov.com.
    You should also check out how other people are using Trident in their expeditions and projects on OpenExplorer. Posting to OpenExplorer is a great way to generate interest, find collaborators, and inspire others about your project. If your project idea benefits the environment, advances science, or does some other good, consider applying to the OpenExplorer Initiatives program. Some projects from the Initiatives program may be eligible to receive tools funded by initiative sponsors.
    When is my Trident going to ship?
    If you are a Kickstarter backer, please check the latest update. If you have pre-ordered Trident, your estimated ship date was sent in an email to you when your card was charged. If your card hasn’t been charged yet, you will receive an email letting you know the approximate ship date when your card is charged. OpenROV will make every effort to ensure you receive your Trident on or before this date. Any changes to this date will be emailed to you along with a full explanation, a new target ship date, and any action needed.

    The most up-to-date information goes into our monthly updates which can be found on our blog.

    Please follow along and let us know if you have any questions.
    When are you going to charge my credit card?
    Credit cards will be charged ahead of delivery date. We will give you two weeks notice before charging your card. You may, at that time or anytime before, change your credit card information or cancel your order.
    Can you charge my credit card right now?
    If you are interested in early payment please send an email to support@openrov.com. Paying in advance won't affect your place in the fulfillment queue or the price of Trident.
    Can I pay using a bank transfer, PayPal, or another payment method?
    If you require a different payment method, send an email to support@openrov.com and we will contact you when it becomes available.
    How do I update my address and/or payment information for a pre-order?
    You can change your shipping address and/or payment information anytime prior to the date your credit card is charged by clicking here and entering your order number and email address.
    What if I want to cancel my order?
    You can cancel your order and receive a full refund any time prior to shipping. Simply follow the link here and click "cancel order." If you would like to cancel your order and have already been charged, you can email us at support@openrov.com and we will issue a refund.
    Is there volume pricing for companies and groups?
    For companies or commercial enterprises we can make arrangements for volume pricing. Please click here and let us know what you’re thinking.
    What about becoming a reseller?
    We don’t have an official policy regarding distribution or reselling yet but we are gathering a list of interested companies to reach out to when the time is right. If you are interested in discussing an opportunity, please click here to start the conversation.
    Do you ship Trident internationally? What import taxes and duties do I need to pay?
    Trident ships to most countries. Several countries have restrictions on shipping high value items to individuals. If you think you are in a country that might have problems, send an email to support@openrov.com and we will look into it with you.

    Duties and taxes are required for importing goods into most countries. Every country has its own set of rules, taxes, and duties. Before ordering you should be aware of the possible charges. There are a few online calculators that can help you estimate your total landed cost but they are not guaranteed to be accurate. We recommend this calculator to get an idea of the costs that you will incur: https://www.simplyduty.com/import-calculator/

    The harmonization code for Trident is: 8525.80.5050. In some instances your country may prefer a different numbering scheme for the last four digits, so if you are having trouble with the first code, try 8525.80.0000.
