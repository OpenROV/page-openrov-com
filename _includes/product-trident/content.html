Trident Puts You in Control

We’ve created an intuitive flying experience that’s easy and fun. Trident works on modern computers with Google Chrome and with Android devices through the OpenROV App.

Trident Is Super Fast & Agile

One of the secrets of its impressive performance is the unique, hydrodynamically offset thruster design. This configuration allows you to move through the water fast and efficiently when you want to rapidly search an area or run a transect, but also allows you to maneuver very delicately when in tight quarters or while looking at a particular target.

Trident Is Durable & Ultra Portable

The side panels are overmolded with a strong, rubber coating, which protects it against impacts and makes it easy to handle when wet. The form factor is small enough to be carried in a backpack or fit under an airplane seat.

Trident Is an Evolutionary Leap in Connectivity

The data connection to Trident is a major evolution for underwater ROVs. It uses a neutrally buoyant tether to communicate to a wireless topside unit on the surface (radio waves don't travel well through water). The topside unit connects to the pilot’s phone, laptop, or tablet using a long range WiFi signal which greatly increases Trident's effective range.

Trident is Flexible & Expandable

Trident is designed to be able to add new sensors, instruments, and payloads. The software that powers Trident is a live open-source project which has been developed for years by both the community and our software team.
