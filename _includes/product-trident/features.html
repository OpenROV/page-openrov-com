Trident Features

  • Ready to Dive

    Trident comes ready to dive out of the box and is easy control with any device.

  • Rated to 100m Deep

    Go deep! Trident dives comfortably to 100m, far deeper than recreational diving.

  • 4 Hours Battery Life

    Onboard rechargeable lithium batteries give you 4 hours of power to explore underwater.

  • Shoot and Record Video in HD

    The underwater-optimized full HD camera offers low latency, high quality video streaming and recording.

  • WiFi Buoy

    Trident uses a neutrally buoyant tether to communicate to a towable WiFi buoy allowing for unprecedented freedom of movement.

  • Expandable and Flexible

    Trident features easy interface for upgrades, add-ons and modules.
