Underwater Research Using Trident
 

Trident is a versatile software and hardware platform built from our maker roots. It comes standard with depth, heading, IMU sensors, and a WiFi buoy with onboard GPS. Trident also uses an all-digital camera and communications system with some of the latest industry standards.
  • Upgradeable and expandable
  • Trident has a mounting standard for new instrumentation, modules, or payloads
  • Communication between Trident and it's peripherals is through a standard wireless protocol
  • Open-source license so developers can create plug-ins
