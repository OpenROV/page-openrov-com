Trident Is an Essential Boat Accessory.

Want to check the set on the anchor or see the condition of a mooring before tying off? With Trident you can easily inspect your boat. Instead of calling a diver, deploy Trident in seconds if you suspect damage to your hull, rudder, or prop. Trident earns its place on board every vessel whether at dock, sea, or anchor.
