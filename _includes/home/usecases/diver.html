Trident Helps You Maximize Your Dive Efficiency

Use Trident to scout your dive conditions and location before you suit up. Trident can dive quickly to 100m and is maneuverable and compact enough to explore deeper and more dangerous areas. During a surface interval, why not use Trident to find a new dive site?
  • Scout and find dive sites
  • Explore up to 100m and more technical locations using Trident
  • Stream live video to friends who can follow along on your dive from the boat
  • Dive masters can keep an eye on everyone from the boat
